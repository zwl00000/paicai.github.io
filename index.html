Overview

Welcome to the PAICAI Workshop website. This workshop aims to bring together researchers, students, and practitioners interested in artificial intelligence, causal reasoning, machine learning, and real-world intelligent systems.

The workshop will feature invited talks, paper presentations, posters, and open discussions on emerging AI research and applications.

Topics of Interest

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Causal Inference and Causal Representation Learning
  • Large Language Models and AI Agents
  • Computer Vision and Multimodal Learning
  • Trustworthy, Explainable, and Responsible AI
  • AI for Healthcare, Education, Science, and Industry
  • Benchmarking, Evaluation, and Real-world Deployment
